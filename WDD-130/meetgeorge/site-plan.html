Overview

Purpose

This site will be a sort of portfolio/brand awareness page for the fictional character George. George represents any character, person, or idea that one may want to center a website around. In addition the website was intentionally made to be ridiculous with a humorous juxtaposition between the classy stylings of the website itself and the simple and even childing content.

Audience

Those of high taste that whish to see the works of my hands, as well as anyone who enjoys sarcasm.
