Riverwood

Riverwood is a small lumber town north east of Falkreath and south of Whiterun and located within Whiterun Hold. Our rout starts just south of town near the Guardian Stones. We then go down stream straight through Riverwood Passing the Bleak Falls Temple on the left and to our right, Skyrim's tallest mountain, the Throat of the World. Our rout ends near Whiterun: home to The Companions, Skyrim's chief adventuring guild, and Dragon's Reach, now home to the Hold's Jar but is rumored to have anciently been used to capture dragons. Don't be scared though, no one seen a dragon in eras and they likely extinct, never to return. This river is filled with History and is a great rout for beginners!

Grizzly Hills

Grizzly Hills is a forested area located in southeastern Northrend and is north of the Howling Fjord. Its home to over 20,000 Grizzlemaw furbolgs, a kind and gentle folk who are native to the area. Our rout takes us down from the Blue Sky Logging Grounds to the Amberpine Lodge. This location is also home to the local pines which are the largest of any of our locations. Keep an eye out for the local animals like wild deer, foxes, and rabbits. In the trees you may even spot a Snowy Owl. There are also local bears, wolves, and frost trolls. So it is recommended you stay in the raft at all times until the end of the rout.

Lethe River

Lethe River is Located in the Sabere Mountains and has two discharge locations. The first into a lake just outside of the small mining town of Narsh. Narsh is also home to the area's famous adventuring school. The other to the Port City of Nikeah, home to many famous pubs and dance halls. No matter where you end up, it will be filled with friendly people. It is absolutely teaming with all sorts of wild life, including local octopi! Whether you want small town charm or big city bustle this river is a great option. It is also fantastic for those who are more experienced in rafting and those adrenaline junkies that want a river filled with twists and turns.
